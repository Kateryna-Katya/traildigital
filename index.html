Roy Story: Match 3 Blast Games

About the Game

Puzzles that move the story forward.
Roy Story: Match 3 Blast Games is built around progression through action. Each completed level contributes to restoring environments and unlocking new parts of the story.
It’s not just about matching tiles — you’re actually rebuilding stuff and making a difference in Roy’s world.

How to Play

Clear steps, smooth learning curve

  • Match Elements

    Combine three or more identical pieces to clear them from the board and start building momentum.

  • Create Combos

    Form larger matches to trigger powerful effects that clear bigger areas.

  • Complete Objectives

    Focus on level goals such as clearing obstacles or collecting specific items.

  • Use Boosters

    Activate special tools when the level becomes more complex.

  • Progress Forward

    Finish levels to unlock new locations and continue the story.

Core Features

  • Designed to keep you coming back for more

  • Increasing level complexity

FAQ

  • Is this a story-based game?

    Yep! Every level you beat actually moves Roy's story forward.

  • Do levels get harder over time?

    Yes. New mechanics and challenges are introduced.

  • Are boosters necessary?

    They become helpful in more complex levels.

  • Can I play in short sessions?

    Yes. Levels are designed to be quick and repeatable.

  • Is the game suitable for beginners?

    Yes. It starts simple and builds gradually.
